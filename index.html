Our Story

Happi Chess Place is not just a chess club, but a cultural home where strategy meets creativity. Open day and night, we've created a sanctuary for thinkers, dreamers, and creators alike.

Founded in 2023, we've quickly become Abuja's premier destination for chess enthusiasts and art lovers looking for a sophisticated yet welcoming environment to connect and create.
